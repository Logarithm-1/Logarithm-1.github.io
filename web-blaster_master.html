Blaster Master

A game I created in 2015 for a project in Introduction to Programing.



Instuctions

  • Click the ball with the mouse.
  • Each time you miss the ball you loss a live.
  • Each time you hit the ball, the ball changes color and moves faster.
  • Five hit, equals a new level.
  • Complete 5 levels to with the game.
  • Hit 'p' to pasue the game.
